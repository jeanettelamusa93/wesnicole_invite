Our Story

Our Beginning & Ever After

Our story began in August 2009, by the click of the “Poke" button on Facebook - a digital nudge to convey each other’s existence.
The "Poke" is one of Facebook's most-cryptic features, but for us, a simple “Poke” 9 years ago was the catalyst for our relationship.
We started chatting and got to know one another, until one night, we happened to be in the same place, at the same time.
We met each other for the first time at the News Cafe. Our first meeting was short, but definitely made an impression, especially after receiving a rose hand made from a paper serviette.
After several months of seeing each other, on 28 April 2012, we went on a date to the News Cafe- this being the place where we first met, followed by a dinner and dance at the "Back of the Moon". When we approached our table, there was a massive bouquet of red roses, alongside a dessert with the words that said “Be Mine”...
Fast forward to 2017 and after 5 years of dating, we took a trip to Australia. On 11.09.2017, ‪we arrived at a place called "Bradley's Head". Hand in hand, and walking down the amphitheater, we came to find the most romantic setting at the end of the pier with the most stunning views over looking the Sydney Harbour and the Sydney Opera House, the perfect setting for the most over the top romantic, out of this world proposal. ‬‬
We can’t wait to get married - we believe that we are soul mates. True love really does exist!
