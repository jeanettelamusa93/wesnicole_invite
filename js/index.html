Our love story

We discovered the meaning of love on a tropical beach in Thailand in 2013.

They say that if you want to truly get to know someone's character travel with them. What caught us most off guard whilst exploring was not the exotic country, rich in culture and cuisine, but the beautiful soul or our travel companion. We experienced that magical spark that everyone searches for, but few find.

Our Thailand trip was over but another adventure was just beginning...

Directions

GPS: S 33.83742 E 18.95320

Directions From the N1

  • Pass the 1st Stellenbosch (Klipheuwel) turnoff
  • Take the R44 / Klapmuts turnoff from the N1
  • Turn Right and drive towards Klapmuts (pass under the bridge)
  • Cross over the first intersection (the R101) (less than 1 km from offramp)
  • Turn Left into the road to Franschhoek and the R45 (less than 1km from the R 101 intersection)
  • Pass Backsberg and drive towards Franschhoek for another 4kms.
  • You will see Fredericksberg (RdeR) on the right hand side of the road.
  • At that point, start slowing down and prepare for a Right turn.
  • Before the railway line crossing, and in front of the thatch cottage, you will see the gates that mark the entrance to Vrede-en-Lust.
